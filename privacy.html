Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

Quick Sticky Notes ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Quick Sticky Notes application.

Information We Collect

Quick Sticky Notes is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary for the app's functionality:

  • Notes Content: The content you create in the app is stored locally on your device and optionally synced through iCloud if you enable this feature.
  • App Settings: Your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device.

iCloud Sync

If you choose to enable iCloud sync:

  • Your notes will be synced across your devices using your iCloud account
  • The sync process is handled by Apple's iCloud service and is subject to Apple's privacy policy
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time through the app settings

Data Storage and Security

All your notes are stored locally on your device and/or in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to your notes or personal information. The security of your data is handled by your device's built-in security features and Apple's iCloud security when sync is enabled.

Analytics and Crash Reports

We may collect anonymous crash reports and analytics data to improve the app's performance and user experience. This data does not include any personal information or the content of your notes.

Third-Party Services

Quick Sticky Notes does not share any data with third-party services except for:

  • Apple's iCloud service (when sync is enabled)
  • Apple's crash reporting and analytics services

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
