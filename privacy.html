Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

Quick Sticky Notes ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Quick Sticky Notes application.

Information We Collect

Quick Sticky Notes is designed with complete privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal data or note content:

  • Notes Content: All notes are stored locally on your device's drive. We never have access to your notes.
  • App Settings: Your preferences and settings are stored locally on your device.

Data Storage and Security

All your notes are stored locally on your device. We do not have any servers or cloud storage - everything stays on your computer. The security of your data is handled by your device's built-in security features.

Analytics and Crash Reports

We do not collect any analytics data or crash reports. The app operates entirely locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

Quick Sticky Notes does not integrate with any third-party services. All functionality is handled locally on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
