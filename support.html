How do I enable iCloud sync?

Open the app preferences, go to the "Sync" tab, and toggle on iCloud sync. Make sure you're signed in to iCloud on your Mac.

Can I customize the appearance of my notes?

Yes! You can customize note colors, fonts, and themes in the app preferences under the "Appearance" tab.

How do I change keyboard shortcuts?

Open the app preferences and navigate to the "Shortcuts" tab where you can customize all available keyboard shortcuts.

Is my data backed up?

If you enable iCloud sync, your notes are automatically backed up to iCloud. Otherwise, we recommend regularly backing up your Mac using Time Machine.
