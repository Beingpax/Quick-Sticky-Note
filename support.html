How do I create a new note?

Where are my notes stored?

All notes are stored locally on your device in the directory you selected during setup. They are saved as markdown (.md) files that you can also use with other apps.

Can I customize the appearance of my notes?

Yes! You can customize note colors and themes in the app preferences under the "Appearance" tab.

How do I change keyboard shortcuts?

Open the app preferences and navigate to the "Shortcuts" tab where you can customize all available keyboard shortcuts.

How do I back up my notes?

Since all notes are stored as local files on your device, you can back them up using Time Machine or any other backup solution you prefer. The notes are stored as regular markdown files in the directory you chose during setup.
